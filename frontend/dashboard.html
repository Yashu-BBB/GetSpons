📖 How to use your Media Kit

Your media kit is your professional pitch to brands. Here's how to get the most out of it.

1
Share it with brands via email

Download your PDF and attach it to any brand outreach email. A polished media kit dramatically increases response rates.

2
Attach it to your pitch emails

When you generate a pitch from the Brands page, always attach your media kit PDF. Brands want to see your stats upfront.

3
Update it regularly as your stats grow

Every time your follower count or engagement rate improves, regenerate your media kit. Fresh stats show brands you're growing.

4
Edit the copy to match your voice

The AI writes a great first draft — but personalise the headline, bio and "Why Partner" section to sound authentically you.
